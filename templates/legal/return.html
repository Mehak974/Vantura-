Return Eligibility

Items may be returned within 30 days of delivery if they are unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition as received. Proof of purchase is required.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned: opened consumables, personal care items after use, downloadable software, and items marked as final sale.

How to Return

  1. Contact us at returns@vantura.com within 30 days
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll send a prepaid return label (eligible orders)
  4. Pack items securely and ship back
  5. Refund processed within 5–7 business days of receipt

Exchanges

Need a different size or variant? Contact us and we'll arrange a free exchange, subject to availability.
